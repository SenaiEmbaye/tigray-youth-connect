About Us

Tigray Youth Connect is dedicated to empowering young people in the Tigray community through education, mentorship, and community engagement. We believe in the power of youth to create positive change and build a brighter future.

Our organization provides resources, support, and opportunities for young people to develop their skills, connect with their heritage, and make meaningful contributions to their communities.
